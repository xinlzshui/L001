常见问题 (FAQ)

Q: 为什么 App 提示需要摄像头权限？

A: TennisBee 需要使用摄像头实时捕捉您的动作进行 AI 分析。由于我们采用本地处理技术，您的视频画面不会被录制或上传，请放心授权。

Q: 离线状态下可以使用吗？

A: 完全可以。所有的识别算法和练习记录都在您的手机本地运行，无需互联网连接，适合在各种球场环境下使用。

Q: 如何获得最佳的识别效果？

A: 建议将手机固定在身体侧方 3-5 米处，确保全身（从头到脚）都在画面内。光线充足、背景相对简单的环境会显著提高识别准确度。

Q: 识别出的持拍手反了怎么办？

A: 您可以在 App 的“帮助中心”页面修改您的持拍手偏好（左手/右手），系统会自动调整镜像识别逻辑。

TennisBee Technical Support

Welcome to the TennisBee Help Center. We are dedicated to providing you with the purest and most professional tennis training experience.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why does the app require camera permission?

A: TennisBee needs camera access to capture your movements for real-time AI analysis. Since we use on-device processing, your video is never recorded or uploaded.

Q: Can I use it offline?

A: Absolutely. All recognition algorithms and exercise records run locally on your phone. No internet connection is required, making it perfect for use on any court.

Q: How do I get the best recognition results?

A: We recommend placing your phone 3-5 meters to your side, ensuring your entire body (head to toe) is within the frame. Sufficient lighting will significantly improve accuracy.

Q: What if the app identifies the wrong racket hand?

A: You can change your racket hand preference (Left/Right) in the app's "Help Center," and the system will automatically adjust the recognition logic.
